About Me

The internet is a window to the world.


Ever since I took my first step on to the world wide web, I was amazed at what I found - ideas, cultures, experiences that would require many trips around the globe, as well as a lifetime, to know otherwise. From that moment, I was hooked, a happy denizen of a connected world.


When I found myself at a crossroads in my life, I decided to step out - far out - of my comfort zone and learn how to make a presence on the web. It was challenging, at times frustrating, but ultimately rewarding. Once I realized that a computer ins't an evil being, its will set against me, but rather a machine, operating according to its own, albeit curious, brand of logic, I stoped worrying and learned to love web design and devopment.
